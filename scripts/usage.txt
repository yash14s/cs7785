I. Turtlebot3 
    1. Bringup [1]
        i. $ ssh ubuntu@{IP_ADDRESS_OF_RASPBERRY_PI}
        ii. $ export TURTLEBOT3_MODEL=burger
        iii. $ ros2 launch turtlebot3_bringup robot.launch.py
    2. For Teleop:
        On PC run:
        $ ros2 run turtlebot3_teleop teleop_keyboard
